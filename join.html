About Bay Royal

Building trust, driving growth, empowering businesses across Africa

Bay Royal International Limited is a trusted partner for organizations across Africa. With a strong presence in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Uganda, Mozambique, and South Africa, we specialize in delivering innovative solutions in Projects, Payments, Payroll, and P.E.O. Our mission is simple: to empower businesses, simplify operations, and create lasting value through professionalism, integrity, and expertise.

  • Seasoned expertise in finance, HR, and project management
  • Client-centric and solution-driven approach
  • Commitment to integrity and excellence
  • Proven track record across Africa
  • Innovative and adaptive solutions
  • Dedicated, professional team
